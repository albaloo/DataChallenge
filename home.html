Bio


I'm Roshanak Zilouchian Moghaddam. Currently, I'm a research scientist at the information school of the University of Washington. I'm working in usegroup under the supervision of Andrew Ko.

I received a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2014. At Illinois, I've been working with Brian Bailey and Wai-Tat Fu on supporting collaboration and consensus building in online user interface design discussions, and with Michael Twidale on challenges of participation in user interface design discussions in open source communities.

My research interests are: Designing and Developing Educational Games, Collaboration and Consensus Building in Online Communities, and Information Visualization
