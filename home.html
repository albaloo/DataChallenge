Data Incubator Challenge Project

Results


I've analyzed Amazon Reviews of Baby Products to answer two questions:
  • Q1. Are there any relationship between length of the reviews and the helpfulness scores given by users?
  • Q2. Are there any relationship between review scores and the helpfulness scores given by users?
In order to find out which features are affecting the helpfulness score, we can perform a regression analysis and find out whether length of the reviews or review scores can predict helpfulness. However, for the purpose of this challenge and because I was asked to deliver plots, I've decided to answer the above two questions by inspecting some graphs. The visual inspection is usually the first step in a complete data analysis process, but it should be enough to demonstrate my skills for the purpose of this challenge.

Q1

Helpfulness score in amazon reviews consists of two numbers [x, y], where x is the number of people who found the review helpful and y is the total number of people who voted for helpfulness or unhelpfulness of the review.
